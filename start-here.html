🛠 How to use LexAi in 4 simple steps

  1. Paste or type any short text (English or other) you want to enhance or translate.
    • Need the original language back? Choose **Rephrase (English-only)**.
  2. Pick your target language or keep English and click **Get Results**. LexAi instantly generates several high-impact alternatives.
  3. Skim the suggestions and tag each one **Good** 👍 or **Bad** 👎. Our model learns from your feedback and surfaces stronger lines over time.
  4. Click **Download → Excel** to grab a tidy file of winners & losers for A/B testing in Facebook, Google Ads or email.
